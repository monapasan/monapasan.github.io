Our Mission
In our high paced and competitive professional environments where teams work under high pressure - stress, conflict and burnout are the top reasons why employees leave their jobs.

Moreover, in the past 10 years in Germany the amount of sick leave days only due to burnout and mental issues increased by 56%.

In order to cultivate cultures that embody professionalism and collaboration, workplaces need to provide their employees with regenerating moments and helpful tools to regulate their stress levels, transcend conflicts, build trust & achieve flow states as an aligned team.

We only work with licensed, experienced practitioners most of them coming from joy space - a healing space and collective in Berlin Mitte. Our highest goal is to provide safe and well paid environments for holistic practitioners on the one hand and high quality services and carefully designed experiences for our clients on the other.

Ola

After some years in various start-ups and web3 companies, Ola left the corporate world to start her healing collective called Joy Space (link) in Berlin Mitte. By now, more than 40 practitioners are part of the healing community. Founding Zen experiences was the next logical step by bringing these kinds of services into the corporate world. Besides her entrepreneurial endeavors Ola is also a licensed Reiki practitioner.

Eliza

Eliza is an innovation consultant and design thinking coach with over 7 years of experience in the corporate world. Through her work in various companies she realized that most of the company environments need new approaches, to be able to survive the fast changing developments of our world. From her point of view, the most important thing for companies to be able to succeed in the future, is to provide their employees with nurturing work environments to help them reach their highest creative potential. Besides Zen Experiences and her freelance career as a consultant she also co-founded lsp-coaches.
