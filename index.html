Our Mission

In our high paced and competitive professional environments where teams work under high pressure - stress, conflict and burnout are the top reasons why employees leave their jobs.

Moreover, in the past 10 years in Germany the amount of sick leave days only due to burnout and mental issues increased by 56%.

In order to cultivate cultures that embody professionalism and collaboration, workplaces need to provide their employees with regenerating moments and helpful tools to regulate their stress levels, transcend conflicts, build trust & achieve flow states as an aligned team.
